Emsalê ke şenê bıgureniyê

Here you may select modules to enable or disable by clicking or unclicking the checkboxes and then hitting the Aktif ke button at the bottom of the page. You may also configure certain modules by clicking on the Awan ke link associated to them if it is present. Some modules also have sub-modules associated with them which may be accessed by clicking on the emsalê bınêni link or on the down-pointing arrow if present.
